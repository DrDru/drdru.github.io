Thanks & Credits (22 / 22)

Thank you for reading this far. I hope you've had a pleasant time. This story embodies my favourite method of learning Japanese : reading a lot of enjoyable comprehensible input.

Most of what I have used to create this story is free (see next page for more details). I encourage you to copy my work and change the dialogues to adapt them to a different audience/language. I encourage you to make your own stories and share them. Making them requires more time and dedication than technical skill.

With enough of them circulating freely the acquisition of a foreign language vocabulary would be a solved problem.
